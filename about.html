ABOUT THE PROJECT

LAstarworks is a long‑term, fully independent project created by Goran Mandić, known under the creative alias Limeniks.

It grew naturally from years of work under Limeniks Artworks – a platform dedicated to visual storytelling, experimentation, and the joy of creating.

Through photography, video production, 3D printing, DIY engineering, STEM exploration, and artistic installations, the idea slowly took shape: to build a personal, private astronomical observatory.

LAstarworks combines:

  • STEM and hands‑on engineering
  • Astrophotography
  • DIY construction
  • Artistic expression
  • Permaculture
  • Storytelling

The observatory will stand in the middle of a garden, surrounded by vegetables, trees, and a permaculture ecosystem.

Every part of the project is created by one person: Goran Mandić (Limeniks).
