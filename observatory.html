OBSERVATORY

The LAstarworks Observatory is a modular, fully custom‑built system designed for portable deep‑sky imaging. It integrates:

  • OpenAstroTracker GoTo mount
  • Carl Zeiss Jena 180mm f/2.8 lens
  • IMX477 and IMX290 camera modules
  • 3D‑printed structural components
  • Custom electronics and power management

The observatory is engineered for precision, mobility, and creative experimentation.
